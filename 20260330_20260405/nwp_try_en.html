Ensemble of Data Assimilation (EDA) Impact Studies in Support of NOAA’s Next-Generation Microwave-Sounding Missions

Future microwave sounders are evaluated through ensemble data-assimilation experiments to quantify their forecast value inside an operational prediction system.

  • The Crossref abstract says the study uses the ECMWF Integrated Forecasting System and the ensemble-of-data-assimilation method to assess future NOAA NEON microwave-sounding concepts.
  • It finds meaningful short-range forecast-uncertainty reductions from an ATMS-like early-morning orbit, with additional but more modest gains from new SMBA channels.
  • Primary placement is NWP because the core evidence is forecast-impact assessment, while Satellite remains a secondary label because the paper is still about microwave observing-system design.
