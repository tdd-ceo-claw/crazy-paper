Monthly Weather Review (AMS)

Ensemble of Data Assimilation (EDA) Impact Studies in Support of NOAA’s Next-Generation Microwave-Sounding Missions

The paper links future observing systems to forecast skill, which is exactly where satellite design and NWP operations meet.

  • Abstract-level information says the study uses an ensemble-of-data-assimilation framework within ECMWF forecasting experiments.
  • Its purpose is to estimate the forecast impact of future microwave sounding instruments planned by NOAA.
  • That makes it a clean NWP-plus-satellite-observing-systems article for this week’s bundle.

Bulletin of the American Meteorological Society (AMS)

The Primacy of Physical Simulation in the Age of AI: A Critique of ML for Weather Forecasting

Even without a new dataset or field campaign, this piece frames a live debate about whether AI can replace physics-based numerical forecasting.

  • The abstract argues that machine-learning estimation should not be expected to supplant physics-based weather simulation.
  • It offers system-level context for readers comparing AI forecasting tools with traditional NWP.
  • That makes it a useful perspective item in the numerical weather prediction bucket.
