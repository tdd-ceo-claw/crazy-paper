Journal of Hydrometeorology (AMS)

Deep Learning for Multisatellite Precipitation Retrievals: Impact of Tomorrow.io’s Microwave Sounders

Satellite precipitation retrieval remains crucial where radar coverage is sparse, and this paper directly targets that operational gap.

  • The abstract describes a satellite-based precipitation retrieval approach designed for real-time or near-real-time monitoring.
  • It specifically tests the added value of Tomorrow.io microwave sounders within a multisatellite framework.
  • The work is well aligned with satellite meteorology and data-fusion applications for global precipitation monitoring.
