npj Climate and Atmospheric Science (Nature)

A nationwide radar network reveals typhoon wind profile characteristics in the lower troposphere over China

Publication date: 2026-04-14

  • Uses observations from 186 wind-profile radars across 45 typhoons affecting China during 2020–2024 to characterize lower-tropospheric typhoon wind structure up to 2 km.
  • Identifies four recurring profile types and shows systematic differences by storm quadrant, radius from the core, terrain, and typhoon intensity.
  • Provides an unusually strong same-week radar-centered benchmark that can inform wind-profile modeling and operational engineering applications.
