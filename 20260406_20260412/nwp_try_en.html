Bulletin of the American Meteorological Society (AMS)

The Operational Forecast Process at MetCoOp

This paper gives a rare operations-focused look at how multiple national services jointly produce regional NWP guidance.

  • The abstract describes a cooperative regional forecast-production system maintained by several Nordic and Baltic meteorological services.
  • Its emphasis is operational NWP workflow, not just model theory.
  • That makes it valuable context for readers interested in how forecast guidance is actually produced and shared.

Journal of Geophysical Research: Atmospheres (AGU)

Subseasonal Forecasting of Snow Cover and Cold Compound Extremes: Insights From MPAS-A Over Midlatitude East Asia

Subseasonal prediction is still a frontier problem, and this paper makes the challenge concrete through snow–atmosphere interactions and compound cold hazards.

  • The abstract evaluates subseasonal forecast skill for snow cover and cold compound extremes in East Asia.
  • It uses MPAS-A and highlights snow–atmosphere interactions as an extra source of forecast difficulty.
  • This is a clean numerical-weather-prediction article at longer lead times.
